The PureWick™ Female External Catheter allows for simple, non-invasive urine output management in female patients. Using low pressure wall suction, the PureWick™ Female External Catheter wicks urine away from the patient and into a designated collection canister. With the PureWick™ Female External Catheter, you now have an option for managing female urinary incontinence.
