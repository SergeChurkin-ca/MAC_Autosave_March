REAL ® Immersive System is an advanced technology using virtual reality (VR) to deliver engaging rehabilitation experiences. Purpose built for healthcare, REAL System offers customizable session and objective tracking.


REAL Activities cover a range of therapeutic and wellness areas. Adjustable settings in TherapyView ™ on the table offer multiple speed, reach distance, and intensity levels to support individual needs of high-acuity to high-functioning patients.


Immerse your patients in a virtual world with activities that support:

Upper Extremity (UE)

  • Range of Motion (ROM)
  • Endurance
  • Strengthening
  • Coordination

Core and Balance

  • Postural control and stability
  • Seated dynamic reaching with rotational trunk control
  • Reaching from base of suppor

Functional Uses, ADL Training

  • Increased independence with self-care tasks
  • Weight shifting
  • Environmental scanning
  • Dynamic reaching

Cognition

  • Awareness of surroundings
  • Decision making during ADLs
  • Memory recall
  • Visual skills addressing visuospatial awareness, attention processing, and sequencing

Distraction

  • Pain and stress management
  • Decreased movement avoidance
  • Tolerance of a busy environment

Low stimulation environments

  • Habituation to new environments
  • Reduced environmental cues
  • Memory recall
  • Promotion of relaxed state
  • Stress management
