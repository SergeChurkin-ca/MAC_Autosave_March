We know your space

Through a deep understanding of the healthcare industry, we tailor Beamery to align with your talent goals, including integrating directly with all of your tools and workflows so you can focus on building world class teams.

Clear, actionable data

With Beamery, data is unified, deduped, enriched and searchable in a single system, with rich integrations to other tools like ATS, recruiting and sourcing tools, and more. Reporting gives easy access to insights and team effectiveness.

Treat candidates like customers

With Beamery, employees are automatically sorted into talent pools for nurture and follow-up, so you can take the same proactive approach as with external candidates. Employees can register their key skills and interests, and receive targeted content and job alerts.
