What do I have to do?

There is no action required of you. The CPower team will contact you in the coming weeks to set up a meeting to ensure a successful transition. Our goal is to provide your company with a hassle-free, seamless transition to CPower. In the meantime, if you have questions on your contract, account support, notifications, payments, reports, statements, or anything else about your account – please see the contact info for your CPower regional Account team below. We have also included a simple form submission at the bottom of this page if you would prefer we follow up with you.


What is the arrangement between CPower and Centrica?

CPower has signed a definitive agreement to purchase Centrica’s U.S. demand response business.


What does this mean for my company and its DR participation?

We aim to provide you with the same great experience you enjoyed with Centrica…and more. The combined technologies and expertise of CPower and Centrica, along with our CPowered™ Technology Solutions that simplifies how our customers communicate, control, and report performance to optimize their DER strategy and investments, means we offer the potential for you to maximize the value of your DERs.


Why was this acquisition made?

CPower’s acquisition of Centrica’s demand response business fits with CPower’s strategy to expand our company’s demand response portfolio both organically and through acquisition. We feel that the customer approach of CPower and Centrica shares the foundation of a deep knowledge, a high level of expertise in the DER market, and a commitment to always putting the customer first.
