Platform Integrations

For our clients (and us!), there's nothing more satisfying than the smooth delivery of a carefully planned and expertly executed integration. The value in hiring an experienced team cannot be understated.

Regardless of scale, a good integration takes both technical acumen and a careful approach to gathering requirements. This is paramount because some integrations (such as CRM) cannot be undone!

It's also important for saving your team time, money, and headaches. MAC is able to sidestep integration pitfalls, and ensure a positive outcome thanks to methods honed through decades of first hand experience and countless integrations.

Additionally, documentation is ALWAYS lacking - you should never set a team member up for failure by asking them to complete an integration based on a documentation resource alone. At the very least, get an expert opinion!

MAC is able to provide first party integrations for certified platforms offered on Hubspot Launch Point, as well as third party product integrations for custom platforms not supported out of the box by Hubspot.
