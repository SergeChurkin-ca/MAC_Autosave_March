WHAT WE DO

HubSpot requires a great deal of time and effort to learn, and even more skill and training to execute the email marketing campaigns, create the landing pages, segment the data, or work through any of the other technical challenges that come with using the HubSpot platform.

Marketing Automation Corp. can help your company get up and running quickly. Rather than spending six months training an internal asset, only to have them leave, we can execute your campaigns from day one and with full continuity.

Areas of specialization include

  • HubSpot inbound marketing
  • Content Strategy
  • HubSpot Filter Configuration
  • Database segmentation
  • HubSpot Integrations

Work directly with your certified HubSpot expert -- no middleman.

All staff in-house (Toronto) for better communication. No off-shoring or outsourcing.

In-house design and coding services.

Full suite of strategic services from industry veterans.

Fast turn around times, experience with many client verticals. Clients across the globe.

Marketing Automation Corp. offers expert HubSpot consultant services.
