What We do

Marketing automation requires a great deal of time and effort to learn, and even more skill and training to execute the email marketing campaigns, create the landing pages, segment the data, or work through any of the other technical challenges that come with using a marketing automation platform.

Marketing Automation Corp. can help your company get up and running quickly. Rather than spending six months training an internal asset, only to have them leave, we can execute your campaigns from day one and with full continuity.

Our team consists of very experienced, certified marketing automation experts. If you chose to work with us, you would:

You would work directly with your marketing automation expert, and be working with the same expert on an ongoing basis.

We offer full flexibility when it comes to billing. There are no mandatory minimums or retainers. Only pay for the time you need, when you need it.

All staff full time,
no outsourcing.

We offer full design and coding services. In addition to emails and landing pages, our team has a great deal of experience creating white papers, banners, posters, and any other design needs you may have.

We offer platform-to-platform services, and have many experts who are certified in transition more than one platform.

Though we are based in Canada, we service clients throughout the world. We've worked with B2B, B2C, and a variety of verticals, including education, tech, and health, just to name three.
