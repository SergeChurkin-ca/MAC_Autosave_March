We know your space

Through a deep understanding of the healthcare industry, we tailor Beamery to align with your talent goals, including integrating directly with all of your tools and workflows so you can focus on building world class teams.

Clear, actionable data

With Beamery, data is unified, deduped, enriched and searchable in a single system, with rich integrations to other tools like ATS, recruiting and sourcing tools, and more. Reporting gives easy access to insights and team effectiveness.

Treat candidates like customers

With Beamery, employees are automatically sorted into talent pools for nurture and follow-up, so you can take the same proactive approach as with external candidates. Employees can register their key skills and interests, and receive targeted content and job alerts.

How we can help
We've created Beamery to solve a lot of the problems you're facing everyday. From proactively building talent pipelines, to helping you make sense of your candidate data.
  • Beamery simplifies the creation of careers sites that perfectly match your brand, while being fully mobile and SEO optimized. Even better, candidates see personalized content and job recommendations.
  • Our easy to use Chrome extension eases the capture of new candidate data, as well as automated sorting of contacts into talent pools for nurture and follow-up.
  • Not only do we have the only Workday Certified CRM integration, but you can integrate Beamery with all of your existing tools and services.
  • We value diversity and inclusion, so we built a proactive sourcing model to actively seek out diverse talent, as well as ways to engage with specific groups with the right content.
