Real-time support

Live chat helps you deliver contextual experiences to your customers in real-time for faster resolutions. Provide immediate support, help your visitors self-serve, and quickly connect through video, voice calls, or cobrowse for even more personalized service.

Be at the right place at the right time

Segment and send messages to your customers based on visitor location, web pages, device type, and other customer behavior. Share files, launch video and voice calls, screen share, or cobrowse right from the messenger.

Route chats to the right team

Don’t know the answer? No need to panic. Easily tag team members to help out with any tricky requests.

Measure and improve customer satisfaction

Easily analyze live chat results to improve your support experience. Track key chat metrics like total chats, chat duration, and customer feedback. Analyze your busiest chat periods, most popular demographics, and top-performing agents.
