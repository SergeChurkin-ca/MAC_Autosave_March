We have some exciting news!

Our 2022 Nelson Scholarship Program is now open!

Do you know a Grade 12 student that is doing great work in the classroom and in their community? This year, we are once again offering two $10,000 scholarship opportunities for students: our Civic Leadership Scholarship and our Indigenous Student Scholarship, in partnership with Indspire.

Recipients of the awards are young leaders of tomorrow, showcasing a commitment to community stewardship and academic achievement.

To learn more about our scholarship program and how to apply, click the link below and be sure to share with your students.
Applications close June 30, 2022.
