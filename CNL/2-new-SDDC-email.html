CNL Strategic Capital Acquires Vektek Holdings
 
 
 
 
 


CNL Strategic Capital is pleased to announce the acquisition of its its eleventh portfolio company, Vektek Holdings (Vektek).
 

Founded in 1974, Vektek is a designer, engineer and manufacturer of automated workholding solutions for CNC machining.
 

Vektek’s products serve a large installed base, are low cost and a minimal component of the customers’ engineering spend and CNL Strategic Capital believes has potential to result in meaningful return on investment for customers and strong performance for Vektek.
