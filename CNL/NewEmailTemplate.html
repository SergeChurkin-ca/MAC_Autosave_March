In light of the recent downturn in the stock and bond markets more advisors are considering private equity as an alternative. As a result, there are a growing number of options to consider in this arena and more to come. How do you choose the right private equity solution for your clients knowing that there is a wide spectrum of product attributes and risk profiles among the options available today?

Join us over lunch to hear from industry veteran, Paul Drury, Senior Managing Director of CNL Strategic Capital. Prior to joining CNL, Paul spent 16 years sourcing and negotiating private equity transactions with Levine Leichtman Capital Partners.

He will discuss:
  • The Ins and Outs of Private Equity: Learn how this asset class has historically performed across market cycles, key trends today, and the various strategies with risk considerations.
  • CNL Strategic Capital: A unique private equity strategy for individual investors that provides the potential for monthly income and long-term growth. 1
