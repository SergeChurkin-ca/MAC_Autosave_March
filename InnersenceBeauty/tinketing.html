You know hair. So you know many haircare brands are formulated with sulfates due to a belief that they provide a more intense cleanse for build-up prone and damaged hair.
We know that sulfates cleanse, and they deliver the lather that makes us believe our hair is getting a deeper clean.
But we also know that over time, they can strip hair of the natural oils and nutrients it needs, leading to frizz, breakage, drying and thinning.
Your hair knows better than most. Give it what it needs.
