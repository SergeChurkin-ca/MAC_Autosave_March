Your Hair Knows Better
 
 
Your hair knows it needs a deep cleanse, but not one that strips away the natural oils and nutrients that help it thrive. Sulfates may clean, but they remove the sebum and oils hair intuitively produces to keep our scalp soft and our strands shining, leaving us facing the same challenges we were trying to remedy.
 
 
 
Your Hair Knows What It Needs
 
 
 
 
When it comes to a cleanse, hair needs a deep clean that lets it stay hydrated and healthy, while still removing the buildup, dirt and toxins that can dull its luster. When we turn to pure active ingredients, we give hair what it knows it needs.
 
Coconut, rice bran and avocado oil extracts deliver intense but gentle cleanses that purify and revitalize hair, sans sulfates.
 
 
 
 
Saturate scalp and strands in pure actives with any of our shampoos, Pure Harmony for fine to medium hair, Hydrate Cream for thick and thirsty hair, or Color Awakening for color and chemically-treated.
