The 4 Ps of SOCs: People, Process,
Platform, & Place

We often have three legged stools in the security world, like the Confidentiality-Integrity-Availability (CIA) triad that is used to build models to quickly convey information in an easy-to-remember way. For security operations centers (SOCs) it is the same, but the stool has four legs, rather than three: People, Process, as well as "Platform" and "Place." In this series of reports, QOMPLX will provide an overview for each of these key components and how they can be leveraged to help your organization build a mature security operations center and incident response function.
